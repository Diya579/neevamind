About NeevaMind

Dedicated to early detection and cognitive wellness support

Our Mission

NeevaMind is committed to providing a gentle, supportive platform for monitoring cognitive wellness and detecting early signs of cognitive decline. We believe that early intervention and consistent monitoring can make a significant difference in maintaining cognitive health.

How It Works

Our platform uses advanced AI technology to analyze your daily reflections, identifying patterns that might indicate changes in cognitive function. By tracking mood, memory clarity, and language patterns over time, we provide insights that help you and your healthcare providers make informed decisions about your cognitive health.

Scientific Approach

Developed with input from healthcare professionals, NeevaMind combines established cognitive assessment methods with cutting-edge AI technology to provide a comprehensive approach to cognitive wellness monitoring.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NeevaMind

Is NeevaMind a medical device?

+

NeevaMind is a wellness monitoring tool, not a medical device. It's designed to help you track patterns and provide insights, but it should not replace professional medical advice or diagnosis.

How secure is my data?

+

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All information is stored securely and we never share your personal data with third parties without your consent.

Can I share my reports with my doctor?

+

Yes! Pro and Family plan users can export their reports and share them with healthcare providers. We provide detailed reports that can be valuable for medical consultations.

How accurate are the AI insights?

+

Our AI insights are based on established research and patterns, but they're meant to be informational rather than diagnostic. The accuracy improves with more consistent usage over time.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

+

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new users, and you'll retain access to your data even after cancellation.
